Socialising

Join a group or take up a hobby

Picking up a hobby is not only a great way to make friends but also a great way to network. If you played a sport at home such as GAA or hurling, I highly recommend joining your local team. You will meet more Irish people and they may be able to help with your job or housing situation. On top of this there is a great sense of community in Canada and Irish people really look out for each other.

Places to have fun

Toronto has some amazing places where you and your friends can have the craic. There is the Rogers Centre where you can go see a baseball game, tickets are generally quite cheap. Canada's wonderland which is Canada's largest theme park is another fun place to visit with your friends. It has some of the craziest rides and it's somewhere you can go back to again and again.

Going out for drinks

Toronto has some excellent and unique bars. If you are into gaming you can visit the Power Up Bar where you can order beers and play computer games. Theres the Rec room which is a large arcade located by the Rogers Centre where you have food, drink and win prizes. Toronto is packed with bars all around the city so your choices are endless.

Events and Festivals

Toronto has some great festivals on, particularly during the Summer. There is Electric Island which is held on the Toronto Islands. You will need to get the ferry across but the fee is small. Electric Island is a great place to enjoy food, drink and music for the whole day. The Budweiser Stadium also holds many of Toronto's biggest concerts. The great thing about the Busweiser Stadium is that it has a great atmonsphere wheter your chilling on the grass or up front by the stage your guarenteed to have an amazing experience.

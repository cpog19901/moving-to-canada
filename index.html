About Me

I recently moved home from Canada but thought I would create this site to give others support on moving to Canada. I moved to Toronto in 2016 and have recently returned home. The move home was not easy as I really enjoyed living in Toronto but I knew it was time to move back. Although I wanted to move back to Ireland I had one of the best experiences of my life living in Toronto. Please read on to view my guide on how to get started on your big move.
